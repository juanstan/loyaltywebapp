About Yalla Rewards
Yalla Rewards is Al Boom Marine’s Loyalty Program that offers discounts and rewards to all our customers, across any of our stores and websites.
How it works
Every time you make a purchase on any of our Group’s stores or websites, you will collect points automatically, which you can then redeem in exchange for discounts, on any future purchases.
Where it’s available
Yalla Rewards is available on any stores and websites of the following brands: Beyond The Beach, Beyond The Bike, Rip Curl, Oakley, Giant Bicycles, Seafolly, Tommy Bahama and more… See all stores >
 FAQs
WHEN DO MY POINTS EXPIRE?
Your points are valid for two years. After this period they will expire.
HOW CAN I KEEP TRACK OF MY POINTS?
Once you complete your registration, you can login to your account and check your points at any time, under the tab “Points & Rewards”.
CAN I USE MY POINTS ON SALES AND SPECIAL OFFERS?
Your points can be redeemed when you purchase items at regular prices and they cannot be used cumulatively, on top of special offers and sales.
CAN I USE MY POINTS ONLINE AND IN-STORE?
Yes, you can earn and redeem points both online and in-store, across the Al Boom Marine’s brands.
